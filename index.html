Mountain bikes

Mountain bikes are specialized vehicles designed for riding on rough terrain, equipped with sturdy frames, wide tires, and enhanced suspension.

Road bikes

Road bikes are lightweight bicycles designed for smooth, paved surfaces, featuring thin tires, aerodynamic frames, and a focus on speed and efficiency.

City bikes

City bikes are comfortable and practical bicycles designed for urban commuting, featuring a relaxed geometry, durable components, and often equipped with accessories like racks and lights.

Electric bikes

Electric bikes are bicycles equipped with a motor that assists with pedaling, making them ideal for longer distances, hill climbing, and reducing the effort required during rides.

Choosing a bike depends on many factors: the type of terrain you'll be riding on, your physical abilities, and your personal preferences.
